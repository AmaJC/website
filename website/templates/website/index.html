Upsilon Pi Epsilon (UPE), Nu Chapter

The premiere Computer Science Honors Society at UC Berkeley.

We help you get stuff done.

In the past we've hosted:
  • Google, Apple, Amazon Infosessions
  • Super Awesome Tech Talks
  • Guest Lecturers such as Elon Musk, Eric Schmidt, etc.

We are looking to extend membership to all prospective majors!

In order to qualify, you must:
  • Be a prospective Computer Science major in the College of Letters and Sciences
  • Have taken at least 2 CS major requirement courses with letter grade B or higher
  • Have taken or currently enrolled in a total of 4 CS major requirement courses
  • Have a CS GPA of at least 3.5

Upholding academic excellence.

Upsilon Pi Epsilon is the first and only international honor society for the Computing Sciences. The UC Berkeley chapter, launched in 1995, honors the top tier of Computer Science students on campus.

We help others.
